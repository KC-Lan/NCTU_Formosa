Parabase: a simple and applicable peptide prediction system with validation of artificial intelligence

Parabase is now a database for antifungal peptides which have the most peptides compared to all databases available in the Internet. To make data easy to search, the relationship between pathogen, disease, peptides is clear on Parabase. Apart from peptides most databases such as NCBI, CAMP have (which could be experiment validated), Parabase tries to find out more peptides with antifungal ability based on Scoring Card Methods. While providing the largest amount of antifungal peptides, we provide a system for all users to test the antifungal activity of all peptides.
